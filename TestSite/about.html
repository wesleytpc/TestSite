About TPC

We exist to turn potential into performance.

Tech Performance Centre is a tech education institution built around one idea: if learners train like professionals, they graduate as professionals. Every programme, project and interaction is designed to reflect the real tech workplace.

The TPC promise

  • Learn from working industry professionals.
  • Work on real projects, not just theory.
  • Grow in both skillset and character.

Our story

Tech Performance Centre was founded to close the gap between traditional education and the realities of the tech industry. Too many graduates left with a certificate but without the confidence, portfolio or professional habits needed to thrive.

We built TPC as a launchpad, not a lecture hall – where learners build real projects, ship work they are proud of, and develop the resilience and mindset of modern tech professionals.

Professionals, not professors

Our facilitators aren’t career academics; they are working professionals who bring real projects, tools and stories into the classroom. That means the skills you build map directly onto the workplace.

  • Facilitators with real industry experience
  • Curriculum built with input from hiring managers
  • Projects that mirror real-world workflows

Our approach to tech education

At TPC, learning is practical, structured and supported – with a clear path from first day on campus to your first day in a tech role.

Skillset + character

We care about more than code or tools. We help you build the habits, communication skills and self-management that make you a strong team member.

Project-based learning

Every module leads to tangible work: applications, case studies and artefacts you can showcase in interviews and portfolios.

Support & accountability

Facilitators, check-ins and a structured learning platform keep you moving forward – even when the work gets challenging.

What it means to be a TPC learner

Being a TPC learner means committing to consistent, sustainable progress. We’ll support you – but we’ll also expect you to show up as the future professional you want to become.

  • Take ownership of your learning journey.
  • Ask questions, seek feedback and iterate.
  • Collaborate respectfully and communicate clearly.
  • Take pride in the quality of your work.

Our values

Excellence

We hold a high bar for the work we ask learners to ship – and for the support we provide.

Integrity

We encourage honest effort, ethical practice and transparency in how we teach and assess.

Growth

We believe skills are built, not born – and that feedback is a critical part of that growth.
